Script #2 Output

This script senses the beginning and end of road trips as well as calculates the total road trip distance. Although the road trip has just three games, there are four flights including the final flight from the last game home. The second and third game are both in Los Angeles which is why the third "flight" has a distance of zero. The first flight to Oakland is longer because Oakland is further West than Los Angeles. See Schedule Comparison for full road trip totals, see the Appendix for every road trip in both the Computer Generated and actual 2018-2019 Schedule.

day away_team road_trip_list road_trip_distance flight distance
