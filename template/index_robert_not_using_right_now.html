Decorations shape

Beautiful collection of tiny and attractive SVG(Scalable Vector Graphics)s to give a unique touch to your page and to make a page stand out.

  • When you are replacing color variables in _user-variables.scss, All SVG shapes color also will be changed accordingly. So you don't need to put extra effort to manage SVG colors.
  • Shape(s) color can also be changed by changing fill helper classes. i.e. Change .fill-primary to .fill-dark
  • Shape(s) position can be changed with Position utility classes.
  • Use .zoom-1, .zoom-2, .zoom-3, .zoom-4, .zoom-n1, .zoom-n2, .zoom-n3, .zoom-n4 to scale shapes.
  • Use .opacity-# classes to set visibility.
  • Use.rotate-10, .rotate-90 and .rotate-270 classes to rotate shapes.
